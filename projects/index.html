Vulkan Renderer/Engine

Vulkan is the modern-era offering from the developers of OpenGL, and offers cross-platform capabilities, multi-threaded processing, and extremely precise manipulation of graphics hardware. Also present is a 3D real-time physics engine, allowing interactions between objects and their environment. This is a work in progress, and is the result of my personal dive into Vulkan via its Java bindings, making this a truly cross-platform application.

Features:

  • Complex model loading via Assimp
  • PBR and Phong rendering models
  • Shadow mapping
  • Vertex-skinned animation
